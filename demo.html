The Muzaffarnagar district court on Wednesday held seven men guilty of killing two people in Kawal village in an attack which is said to have triggered the 2013 Muzaffarnagar riots that led to killing of over 60 people.

The court convicted Muzammil, Mujassim, Furkan, Nadeem, Jahangir, Afzal and Ikbal for killing Gaurav and Sachin on August 27, 2013 and rioting. The two youth were murdered allegedly after a brawl that started after their two-wheeler collided with the bike of locals.

The quantum of sentences will be pronounced on Friday.
